About
-----

This a series  is intended for developers who want to learn how to create their
own packages in ROS 2.
It is not meant for people who want to use ROS 2 with it's existing packages.

Prequisites
-----------

- `Install ROS (Dashing or later) <../Installation>`

- `Install colcon <https://colcon.readthedocs.io/en/released/user/installation.html>`__

- Setup your workspace by sourcing your ROS 2 installation.
